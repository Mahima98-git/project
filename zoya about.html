Zoya' Fashion

ZOYA fashion house is co-founded by us, Rosina and Alissa, a mother-daughter duo of Indian heritage, whose primary aim is to reinvent how people experience slow fashion. Although Indian by origin, we have made a home for ourselves in Europe, where we were born, in Africa where our ancestry is rooted, and the Middle East where we currently reside.
Zoya is a chain of luxury diamond boutiques from the House of Tata. It is one of three brands of the jewellery division of Titan Company Limited. It is both a product and a retail brand and is created for the design-seeking woman with an international sensibility. ZOYA BOUTIQUE. MUMBAI.
Our primary aim is to reinvent how people experience slow fashion. Abayas, Sarees, Masks – we make what the market calls for and integrate it with our extensive knowledge of fabric, cut and culture. Every garment that we produce is carefully, ethically and sustainably made in house with the highest attention to detail and craftsmanship. At ZOYA we have something for every woman….
